First Post (unspecified date)

Dear Diary,


Oh man, where do I begin. Well for starters, I'm barely alive! Yea, would you bellieve me if I told you, that a mae boy, nay, a BABY defied my powers and now I'm the one dying!? But, I'm trying to stay positive-met a friend today. His name is Phlem. He is just so happy about everything. I'm trying to be more like him. Well have to get going our wagon leaves tomorrow for Oregon! Can't wait!!!

Xoxo Voldy

Until next time
